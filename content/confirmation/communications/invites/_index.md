**Recruiting next year's class.**

These go out in the spring — usually to the rising 9th-grade confirmation list (current 8th-graders). We send both: an email invite for families who are already in the digital loop, and a physical mailing for families who aren't. Both should arrive the same week.

{{< callout type="info" >}}
The mailing includes the same content as the email, but with a little more warmth and a printed calendar preview on the back. Parents save paper; kids don't read email.
{{< /callout >}}
