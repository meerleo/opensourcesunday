**The email we send to invite rising 9th graders.**

Replace the bracketed bits with your church name, dates, leader names, and registration link.

---

**Subject:** Confirmation Class at [Church]!

> Dear Student and Parent/Guardian,
>
> We are delighted to invite you to be a part of the **2025–2026 Confirmation class** at [Church Name]!
>
> Confirmation class is an opportunity for students to strengthen their faith and learn what it means to follow Jesus in the real world. It culminates in an opportunity to make a public profession of faith and become a member of [Church Name].
>
> Parents are expected to play an active role during their child's Confirmation journey. This includes regularly attending church, attending select classes, participating in conversations about life and faith with their children, and engaging in spiritual practices with their families.
>
> Confirmation class is designed for students in 9th grade or above and it will meet on Sundays from 9:45–10:45am at [location]. Come early for bagels. **The first class kicks off on October 5th.**
>
> Students are expected to commit to attending all classes and activities. The year includes ~24 Sunday morning classes and a few extra events. We do understand that some absences are unavoidable.
>
> Please prayerfully consider your decision to join Confirmation and feel free to reach out with any questions or concerns. If you decide to attend, please fill out the registration form linked below.
>
> We are excited at the possibility of journeying with you through the next year.
>
> In Christ,
> The Confirmation Teachers
