**Sent in the final weeks of the year, before the statement-of-faith deadline.**

---

> Hello Confirmation Parents,
>
> Can you believe that we are just weeks away from our students being confirmed? As our confirmands begin to write their **statements of faith, we need your help.**
>
> 1. Please make yourself available to help your children articulate their faith as they write their statements. **This should be their decision and ultimately their thoughts,** but as a teenager — or anyone, for that matter — this can feel like a daunting task. We are expecting a rough draft by our next class on **5/4.** See the attached document for some guidance.
> 2. Please send me **3–4 pictures by 5/1** of your child, spanning from when they were a baby up until now.
> 3. Mark your calendars for the **Confirmation Dessert, 7pm at [location] on Thursday 5/15.** After students read aloud their statements of faith, the elders will vote on the confirmands becoming members of the church.
> 4. Mark your calendars for **Confirmation Sunday at 9:10am on Sunday 6/1** at [Church]. The students will complete their confirmation process in front of the larger congregation during the 9:30 worship service. Please invite your families to be there.
>
> Confirmation is an important time in the faith formation of our teens. **While there is an end to the confirmation process, this is more of a celebration than a graduation.** It is only one step in the lifelong faith journey of our students, and your participation in that faith journey is vital to them developing a lasting and impactful faith.
>
> Please let me know if you have any questions.
