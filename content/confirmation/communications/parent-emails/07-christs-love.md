> **Recap:** We just finished up unit seven, where we discussed the question: **What does it mean to show Christ's love?**
>
> There are two different Greek words in the New Testament that are translated as *love.* There is **phileo** — a warm and affectionate love. And there is **agape** — a sacrificial, action-oriented love. Both loves are necessary for Christ-followers, but *agape* takes the highest priority, because agape is the sort of love God showed us through Jesus' giving his life for ours.
>
> As followers of Jesus, we respond to Christ's love by **putting our love into action** and serving those around us. For our second class, we put Christ's love in action by packing food bags for people living in motels.
>
