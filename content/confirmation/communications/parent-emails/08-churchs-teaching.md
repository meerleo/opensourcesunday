> **Recap:** We just finished up unit eight, where we discussed the question: **Will you devote yourself to the church's teaching?**
>
> The church's teaching comes first and foremost from the **Scriptures.** Part of following Jesus means immersing ourselves in our Holy-Spirit-inspired Bibles. The Bible teaches us both about who God is and who *we* are. The church has also written **creeds and confessions** over the course of history to concisely articulate the truths we find in Scripture. The most famous creed, shared across virtually every Christian denomination, is the **Apostles' Creed.**
>
> Over the last two weeks we also discussed the significance of **weekly intergenerational worship.** Aside from Sunday School, one of the primary places we learn about God is in the weekend worship service. The components of a Presbyterian worship service are selected and aligned with intentionality, and taken right from the pages of Scripture.
