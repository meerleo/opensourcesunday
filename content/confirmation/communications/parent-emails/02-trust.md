> **Recap:** These last two weeks we wrestled with the question, *how do we know and trust God?* If we are going to trust God, then we have to seek to *know* God. As we build a relationship with God and learn about how God has acted in the past through the Scriptures, we can learn to trust him even during times of uncertainty. **To know God is to trust God** — and to trust God is a tremendous blessing in every area of our lives.
>
> **Your turn:** This week, watch this short video with your child. *[Video link.]*
>
> Then share and ask these questions:
>
> - Share what it looks like in your own life to trust God. Make sure to share your struggles too.
> - Ask: when is it easy for you to trust God? When is it difficult?
> - Ask: what is one way you can know God better so that you might trust him more?
>
> Remember, you are not expected to know all the answers — simply to journey with your child in this faith-forming process. Even just taking ten minutes out of your day to engage with your child in this way is incredibly important in helping them develop a faith that lasts.
