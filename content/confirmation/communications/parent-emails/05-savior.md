> Hello Parents,
>
> **Important Dates:**
>
> - Confirmands are expected to join youth group on **Tuesday, 12/17 from 6:30–8:30pm.** Parents are encouraged to join as well.
>
> **Recap:** We just finished up unit five, where we discussed the question: **Who is my Savior?**
>
> In the world we are inundated with "saving" promises. Ads tell us that different products will save us from having frizzy hair to suffering the terrible fate of being irrelevant. Yet, there is only one who is truly able to save. Out of love, God sent Jesus into the world to save us from our sin and separation from God. **The best news of all: we don't *earn* salvation — we *receive* it.** It is God's gift to us. Salvation means spending eternity united with God in heaven, but it also means freedom, healing, wholeness, forgiveness, and abundant life here and now.
>
> **Your turn:** Read **Ephesians 2:1–9** together.
>
> Parents, share your own salvation story:
>
> - How has God changed your life?
> - In what ways have you experienced God's love and grace?
>
> Then ask:
>
> - How does it make you feel knowing that God sent Jesus to save us *"out of love"?*
> - Do you ever try to *earn* God's love and salvation?
> - How can we as a family remind one another of God's unlimited grace and love for us?
>
> You don't need to have all the answers. A ten-minute conversation can impact your child's faith for a lifetime.
