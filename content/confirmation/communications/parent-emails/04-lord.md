> Hello Confirmation Parents,
>
> **Announcement:** There will be no Confirmation class this Sunday, 11/17. Please pray for our retreat.
>
> **Recap:** We just finished up unit four, where we discussed the question: **Who is your Lord?**
>
> In **Philippians 2:6–11,** the Apostle Paul provides us with a beautiful hymn that describes Jesus as the Lord of all creation — a Lord who uses his infinite power for the sake of others, and who ultimately gave his life for ours. **John 10:10** reminds us that Jesus is a Lord who desires to give us *abundant life.*
>
> As Lord of the universe, we are called to invite Jesus to be Lord over every aspect of our lives. In all our actions and decisions, we are either declaring the lordship of Christ, or we are denying it. To deny the lordship of Jesus is to deny the abundant life he offers us. Who would want to miss that?
>
> **Your turn:** This week we encourage you to read **Philippians 2:1–11** and **John 10:10** with your child.
>
> - Share the ways that you seek to live your life in a way that declares the lordship of Jesus.
> - Share the areas of your life where it is easy for you to deny Jesus' lordship.
> - Work together with your child to brainstorm ways that your family can have the same mindset as Jesus.
> - Close by praying together that your lives would declare the lordship of Jesus to the world.
>
> **"Who is your Lord?"** I encourage you to work this question into the very fabric of your family. When you are struggling with a decision, when your child is wondering how to respond to a particular circumstance, might you audibly ask together, *"Who is our Lord?"*
