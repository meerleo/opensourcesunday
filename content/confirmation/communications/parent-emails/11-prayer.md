> **Recap:** The last two weeks we answered the big question: **Will you devote yourself to prayer?**
>
> As followers of Jesus, we are to live lives of prayer. **1 Thessalonians 5:17** says *"Pray without ceasing."* Prayer is both a **posture** we embrace and a **practice** we implement in our own lives. In a posture of prayer, we see all that we say, do, and think as being part of our ongoing conversation with God. In our practice of prayer, we intentionally communicate and commune with God in specific ways. The **Lord's Prayer** is both the primary posture and practice Jesus demonstrated for us.
>
> **Your turn:** Of course we encourage you to pray *for* your family — but as you are helping to form the faith of your children, we want you to commit to praying *with* them. Some ideas:
>
> - Share prayers at meals
> - Pray before bed or first thing in the morning
> - Pray after games / activities
> - Write a family prayer together
> - Say the Lord's Prayer together
> - Pray pray pray
>
> Whatever you decide to do — **commit to it, model it, and invite your kids into it.** The lives of your children will be tremendously blessed for it.
