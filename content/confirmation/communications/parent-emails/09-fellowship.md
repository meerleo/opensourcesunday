> **Recap:** We just finished up unit nine, where we discussed the question: **Will you devote yourself to the church's fellowship?**
>
> The Greek word for fellowship in the New Testament is **koinonia.** Koinonia is *not* just superficially hanging out — it's truly **doing life together.** This means sharing joys and burdens, caring for one another, and using our gifts to work together to advance God's mission of reconciling the world. The koinonia of the church is like being members of the same body. Each member of the body is unique — and this diversity allows the body to function in magnificent ways. At the same time, if one member of the body suffers, every member suffers with it.
>
