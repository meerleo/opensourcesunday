**A simple attendance tracker.**

One row per student, one column per session. A few extra columns for retreat attendance, Serve Saturday, and the Confirmation Dessert.

## Columns

- **Student name** *(preferred name for day-to-day, plus a separate full-name column for official records)*
- **Each session date** *(1A, 1B, 2A, 2B, ... 12)*
- **Fall retreat**
- **Serve Saturday**
- **Confirmation Dessert**

## How to use it

- Mark **P** (present), **A** (absent), or **E** (excused) each Sunday.
- At the end of January and again in April, take five minutes with the roster — any student with more than 3 unexcused absences gets a phone call. **Pastoral, not punitive.**
- The tracker is also useful when a parent asks in April: *"How many classes has my kid missed?"* and you can answer without guessing.
