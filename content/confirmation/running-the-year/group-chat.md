**How we run the Confirmation group chat.**

A group chat is the single most useful tool for keeping a Confirmation class connected between meetings. We use a purpose-built chat inside our church's app so that every thread has an adult in it, parents can be looped in, and messages stay accessible to both students and leaders.

## Instructions for Students

Hand this out at Kickoff (or screenshot and text to the class chat if it already exists):

1. **Download the church app.** Search for your church name in the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
2. Press **Get** or **Download.**
3. Open the app and click the **person icon** in the top-right corner.
4. Press **"Log in or sign up"** and create an account.
5. Once signed in, return to the home screen and press the **messaging icon** in the top-right corner.
6. Press **Discover** and search **Confirmation.**
7. Click the **Confirmation** group, click **Join Group,** and then **View in Messaging.**
8. The group chat will now be available under **Conversations.**

## If Your Church Doesn't Have an App

Common alternatives:

- **GroupMe** — platform-agnostic, doesn't require a phone number to invite.
- **A private Discord server** — easy for leaders to moderate channels; overkill for smaller classes.
- **Signal group** — simple, secure, but requires a phone number.

Whichever you use: **a leader is in the group, parents know it exists, and safe-practices guidelines are followed.**

## Ground Rules for the Chat

Write these up in the first message when the group is created:

- A leader is always in this chat.
- Talk like you would in front of an adult — because you are.
- No bullying, no one-on-one DMs between adults and students.
- Use the chat to share prayer requests, schedule reminders, and questions from class.
