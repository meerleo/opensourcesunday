**Student roster and parent contact templates.**

Two simple spreadsheets the lead teacher keeps current throughout the year.

## Student roster

One row per confirmand:

- **Full name** *(including middle name — needed for Confirmation Sunday)*
- **Preferred name** *(what they actually go by)*
- **Grade / school**
- **Baptism status** — baptized already, or to be baptized at Confirmation
- **Baptism date** *(if known)*
- **Mentor** *(assigned by late October)*
- **Attendance** — link to the [attendance sheet](../attendance/)
- **Statement of faith** — draft / revised / final / read aloud
- **Photo collection** — Y/N (for the Confirmation Sunday slideshow)

## Parent contacts

One row per confirmand household:

- **Student name**
- **Parent(s) / guardian(s) name**
- **Phone(s)**
- **Email(s)**
- **Preferred contact channel** — text, email, app
- **Household notes** — divorced, shared custody, grandparent-in-the-loop, etc. *(Keep this respectful and minimal.)*

## Privacy

This data is **pastoral** and should be treated as such. Don't share it outside the Confirmation team. At the end of the year, archive in a secured location and clear the working copy.
