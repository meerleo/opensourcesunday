**The operational playbook for leading a year of Confirmation.**

The lessons are the heart of Confirmation, but they don't fly without a calendar, a roster, a group chat, and two adult guides (one for the lead teacher, one for student mentors). This is the scaffolding every Confirmation director wishes had been handed to them on day one.

## In this section

- **Sample Calendar** — a full-year schedule, student-facing and parent-facing
- **Roster & Parent Contacts** — spreadsheet templates for the year
- **Attendance** — a tidy attendance tracker
- **Group Chat Setup** — how we do the student group chat and why
- **Teacher's Guide** — the lead teacher's overview of the whole curriculum
- **Mentor's Guide** — *Big God, Big Questions* — what to hand each adult mentor

{{< callout type="info" >}}
Every document in this section is a **template.** Copy it, change the dates, swap the names, and use it. The only thing church-specific in these files is the church name, and those are called out.
{{< /callout >}}
