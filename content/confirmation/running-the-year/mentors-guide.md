**For each mentor.**

Every confirmand gets an adult mentor for the year — someone from the congregation who commits to meeting, praying for, and eventually speaking over them on Confirmation Sunday. This guide is what we hand every mentor at the start of the year: their job description, conversation starters, and monthly rhythms.
