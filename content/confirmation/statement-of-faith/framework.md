**How to help a 9th grader write a statement of faith.**

Three pieces of scaffolding we put in front of students during the last few weeks of Confirmation:

1. **The Apostles' Creed** — the minimum shape of Christian belief. If it's not addressed somewhere in the student's statement, something's missing.
2. **The "Big 4" Questions** — God, Jesus, the Spirit, the Church. Simple prompts to get thoughts on the page.
3. **The Writing Guide** — a page-by-page walk through the drafting process.
