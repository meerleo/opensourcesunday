**The credo project.**

Every confirmand writes their own statement of faith during the final weeks of Confirmation and reads it aloud on Confirmation Sunday. It's their own words, not their parents', not the church's — what they actually believe and why.

**The examples are to prime the pump, not to template.** The whole point of this project is that it's *their* words. A student whose statement parrots a past example has missed it entirely — lovingly send them back to the drawing board.
