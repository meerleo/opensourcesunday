**Confirmation Sunday, confirmands leading.**

Instead of the pastoral staff running the whole service, the confirmands do. One student preaches. Others read Scripture. All of them read their statements of faith aloud and are confirmed in front of the gathered church. It's the liturgical capstone of the year.
