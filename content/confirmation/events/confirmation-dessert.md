**The end-of-year celebration.**

The week before Confirmation Sunday, families, mentors, and pastors gather for dessert. Each confirmand is introduced by their mentor, who speaks a blessing over them in front of the room. It's low-key, it's emotional, and it's one of the most important nights of the year.
