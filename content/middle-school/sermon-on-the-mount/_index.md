**A nine-week series through the Sermon on the Mount.**
**Scripture anchor:** Matthew 5–7
**Series ONE thing:** Jesus doesn't just want us to follow the rules harder — he's inviting us into a brand new way to be human, built on him.

{{< callout type="info" >}}
**Leading this series?** The nine lessons are designed as one arc — identity first, then witness, heart, prayer, treasure, trust, judgment, asking, and finally foundation. Lesson 9 builds an explicit series recap into the teaching, so the ordering matters. If you only have time for a shorter arc, at minimum do Lessons 1 and 9 together.
