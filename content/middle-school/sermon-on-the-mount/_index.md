**A four-week series through the Beatitudes.**
**Scripture anchor:** Matthew 5:1–12
**Series ONE thing:** Jesus flips the world's scoreboard upside down — the people the world overlooks are the ones God calls *blessed*.

## The arc

{{% steps %}}

### Week 1 — Up the Mountain

The setting. The crowd. The first three Beatitudes. Jesus starts his most famous sermon by cheering for all the wrong people.

### Week 2 — Blessed Are the Hungry

What's underneath. Craving, mercy, and being the same person in every room.

### Week 3 — Blessed Are the Brave

When following Jesus costs you something. Peacemakers and the persecuted.

### Week 4 — Living the Blessed Life

The reveal: every Beatitude is a portrait of Jesus himself. A commitment, a closing blessing, and sending.

{{% /steps %}}
